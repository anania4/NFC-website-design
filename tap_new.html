WHAT IS IT?

Tap Card is a modern, reusable business card that lets you share your contact information instantly. Simply tap or scan to send a customizable profile with your links, social media, and other important content. No paper, no hassle—just a smarter, faster way to connect.

How It Works

1

Tap or Scan

Simply tap your TAP card to any smartphone or scan the QR code to instantly share your digital profile.

2

Digital Profile

Your complete digital business card appears on their phone - no app needed, works on any device.

3

Save & Connect

They save your contact directly to their phone with one tap. Networking made effortless.
